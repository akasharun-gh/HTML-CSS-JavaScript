Chicken

This dish is considered a type of Peruvian comfort food, comprising chicken cooked with yellow chili peppers, walnuts, spices, garlic, and turmeric. The dish uses aji amarillo peppers, which are yellow, mildly spicy peppers.

Beef

This meaty weeknight casserole channels those ground beef tacos you loved as a kid. It has a cheesy beef and bean base laced with warm spices, and is topped with lettuce, tomato, and creamy Greek yogurt.

Sushi

Sushi is a dish containing sushi rice, cooked, white rice, flavored with seasoned rice vinegar. Sushi is the most famous Japanese dish outside of Japan, and one of the most popular dishes inside Japan, as well.
